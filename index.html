About me
I’m a web developer, designer, and video editor with a strong interest in technology and digital experiences. I started coding at 14 and have been building and refining my skills ever since. I specialize in custom Framework7 applications, focusing on performance, usability, and clean, modern design. I pay attention to detail to make sure everything feels smooth and intuitive. Inspired by the design philosophies of Huawei and Apple, I aim to create work that is simple, polished, and effective.
